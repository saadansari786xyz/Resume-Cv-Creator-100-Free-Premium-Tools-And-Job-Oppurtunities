Frequently Asked Questions

Is this resume builder really free to use?

Yes, our AI-powered resume builder is completely free to use with no hidden charges. You can create, edit, and download as many resumes and cover letters as you need.

How does the AI help with my resume?

Our AI analyzes your input and job description (if provided) to suggest optimal phrasing, highlight relevant skills, and tailor your resume and cover letter to stand out to employers.

Can I edit my resume after generating it?

Absolutely! All generated resumes and cover letters are fully editable. You can make changes directly in the preview before downloading.

What file formats can I download my resume in?

Currently, we support PDF downloads which is the most widely accepted format for job applications. PDFs maintain your formatting across different devices and systems.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. All processing happens in your browser - we don't store your personal information on our servers.
